Our Services

Comprehensive Spine & Rehabilitation Solutions

From advanced decompression therapy to biometric health monitoring — every treatment is evidence-based, personalised, and designed for lasting outcomes.

Spine Rehabilitation & Physiotherapy Services

Non-Surgical Spinal Decompression

Advanced motorised decompression therapy that gently stretches the spine, relieving disc pressure and nerve pain without surgery or medication.

Robotic & Digital Rehab

AI-guided robotic rehabilitation systems for precision movement recovery, combined with digital monitoring for real-time progress tracking.

Pain Management

Multi-modal pain management combining TENS, ultrasound therapy, dry needling, and cupping for comprehensive pain relief and recovery.

Biometric Health & Paleo Tree

Comprehensive biometric profiling integrated with functional nutrition strategies to support spine health from the inside out.

Advanced Manual Therapy

Hands-on specialist manipulation, mobilisation, and soft tissue techniques tailored to individual spine conditions and recovery goals.

Geriatric & Pediatric Rehab

Age-specific rehabilitation programmes designed for children with developmental conditions and elderly patients with degenerative spine disease.
