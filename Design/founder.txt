Founders
1.	Dr. Pranav Kumar (PT)
Founder & Director – ProSpine Healthcare Private Limited
Visionary Leader in Advanced Spine Rehabilitation, Preventive Healthcare & Integrated Wellness
Dr. Pranav Kumar (PT) is the Founder and Director of ProSpine Healthcare Private Limited and the visionary force behind Bangalore’s flagship Pro Spine Centre. With more than 21 years of clinical excellence in Orthopaedic Manipulation Therapy and Spinal Rehabilitation, he has established a strong reputation for delivering evidence-based, non-surgical spine care solutions that combine advanced technology with precision manual therapy. His professional journey reflects a deep commitment to innovation, patient-centered care, and the advancement of modern rehabilitation practices.
A Master in Physiotherapy in Orthopaedics with an MBA in Human Resource Management, Dr. Kumar brings together strong clinical expertise and strategic leadership. His practice is shaped by advanced training in globally recognized manual therapy approaches including Cyriax, Maitland, McKenzie, Mulligan, Myofascial Release, Neurodynamics, and advanced Robotic Spinal Decompression Techniques. At Pro Spine, he has developed an integrated model of care centered around Non-Invasive Robotic Spinal Decompression Therapy supported by structured rehabilitation protocols. This approach is further strengthened by preventive spinal health solutions using 2D and 3D spinal scanning, postural analysis, and digital muscle strength measurements, reflecting his belief that modern rehabilitation must focus not only on treatment, but also on prevention and long-term functional wellness.
Over the years, Dr. Kumar’s contributions have earned prestigious recognition. He received the Best Clinician Award from All India Institute of Medical Sciences in 2023 and from Banaras Hindu University in 2024, in addition to the Doctor’s Pride Award in 2024. These honors reflect his excellence in clinical outcomes, innovation in rehabilitation, and dedication to improving patient care.
Driven by a broader vision of healthcare, Dr. Kumar is currently pursuing his third Master’s degree, M.A. in Psychology, with a special focus on addressing the growing mental health challenges among India’s working population. His aspiration is to evolve as a Mental Health Coach who integrates physical rehabilitation with psychological resilience, creating a holistic mind-body model of care. This vision aligns with the growing need for integrated healthcare solutions in modern corporate India and reflects his belief that true wellness extends beyond physical recovery.
Dr. Kumar is also recognized as the initiator of the “Office Syndrome” concept in India, a pioneering effort aimed at addressing the rising burden of musculoskeletal disorders, lifestyle diseases, postural dysfunctions, and stress-related health concerns among working professionals. Through this concept, he has contributed significantly to occupational health awareness and positioned Pro Spine as a thought leader in preventive and corporate wellness.
As a healthcare entrepreneur and visionary leader, Dr. Kumar is shaping Pro Spine into a future-ready, outcome-driven, and investor-aligned healthcare venture with strong expansion potential. His long-term vision is to build an integrated healthcare ecosystem that combines advanced spine care, rehabilitation technology, preventive health, and mental wellness. Through this mission, he continues to position Pro Spine as a modern, scalable model of holistic healthcare designed for the evolving needs of contemporary society.
